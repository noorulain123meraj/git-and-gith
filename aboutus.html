About Eid Festivals

Eid ul Fitr

Eid ul Fitr is a significant religious holiday celebrated by Muslims worldwide that marks the end of Ramadan, the Islamic holy month of fasting. The festival is a time for special prayers, feasting, and giving charity to those in need. Muslims wake up early on this day, dress in their best clothes, and head to the mosque for prayers.

Eid ul Adha

Eid ul Adha, also known as the Feast of Sacrifice, honors the willingness of Ibrahim (Abraham) to sacrifice his son as an act of obedience to God. However, God provided a ram to sacrifice instead. Muslims around the world sacrifice animals to commemorate this event and share the meat with family, friends, and the less fortunate.
